How to Open

To open the game download the zip file. Then click on the index.html file. The game should then load in your web browser.

How to Play

Upon opening the game you will be greeted with a landing page. You can select the player icon to choose the players representation. After that the game will begin. Use the arrow keys to avoid the roaches and try to get to the water. After your first move the countdown will begin.

When you get to the water you win and your score will be recorded and if your score is in the top three it will be recorded.

If you collide with a roach you will lose a life and the game will restart. If you lose all of your lives a new screen will apear and you can click to start again.
